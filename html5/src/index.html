    Rules

    Try to pack falling tetromino shapes as tightly as possible on the grid. Move the falling piece left or right, rotate it clockwise or anti-clockwise. Completed horizontal lines are cleared and remaining pieces fall down. The game ends when a new piece cannot be placed.

    Controls:

    • ← / → – move left / right
    • Z / X – rotate left / right
    • ↓ – soft drop one row
    • Space – hard drop to bottom
    • P – pause / resume

    Have fun!

    About the Game

    A Tetromino is a geometric shape composed of exactly four equal-sized squares joined edge-to-edge. The concept of stacking such shapes is in the public domain.
